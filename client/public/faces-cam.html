👁️ Webcam face tester (dev only)

Stands in for the Pi vision sidecar: your webcam → an in-browser face embedding → the same /api/faces/observe endpoint. Turn Familiar faces ON in the console first. Run this a while to enroll (snapshots get banked), assign your cluster to a child in the console, then keep watching — the kiosk switches to that child. All in-browser; nothing is uploaded externally (faces post only to your local server).
